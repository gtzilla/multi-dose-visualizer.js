Multi Dose

Taking the same dosage every day is simple. But, what happens when your medication requires special patterns? What happens if you need a different dosage once a week or twice a week? What about if you need a different dosage every 5 days? Use this medication dosage calendar, multi-dose, to find out. It's easy to visualize not only how frequently a variant dosage should be taken, but also if you are still aligned with your pattern.

Privacy: This app collects no telemetry and doesn't track users behavior. Dosage pattern and start date are stored locally in your browser and is never shared.
